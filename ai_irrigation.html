Overview

This system uses artificial intelligence to analyze weather data, soil moisture, and crop type to optimize irrigation schedules, saving water and enhancing crop yield.

Components

  • Raspberry Pi
  • Camera Module
  • Soil Moisture Sensor
  • AI Model (trained for crop-specific irrigation)

How It Works

The AI analyzes input from sensors and weather forecasts, determining the optimal time and amount of water to deliver to each plant.
